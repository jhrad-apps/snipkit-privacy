Privacy Policy

Snipkit for macOS

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how Snipkit handles your data.

Data Storage

Snipkit stores all your snippets locally on your device. Your data never leaves your Mac.

Data Collection

We do not collect, transmit, or have access to:

  • Your snippets or their contents
  • Personal information
  • Usage analytics
  • Any other data from your device

Third-Party Services

Snipkit does not integrate with any third-party services or analytics platforms.

Data Security

Your snippets are stored in a sandboxed container protected by macOS. Other applications cannot access this data. Please note that Snipkit is not intended for storing sensitive information such as passwords or financial data.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the date below.
